How Quest Works

Quest's Features are Designed to help you set up and run your Reputation and Rewards Structure.

Badges

Issue badges to acknowledge a member’s contribution, participation, or engagement. Badges can be added manually or set up to be issued automatically based on Connected Data Sources.

Cards

Badges get added to the member’s Community Dynamic NFT, known as a Card, that evolves as a Member continues to contribute and build their community reputation.

Levels & XP

Set up levels, based on XP, for a member to complete and receive incentives or rewards. With each level up the Card Theme can be set up to evolve.

Profile & Directory

Profile is a place for Members to view Communities, their members, Badges to Claim and showcase their level, XP and skills.

Use Cases

Some of the ways in which your Community and use Quest to Grow.
Gating

Grant Privileges such as gated access to content, channels, links and events based on Badges held or XP Scores.

Privileges

Roles and Permissions can be assigned to users based on Reputation accrued in a Community and on PLatforms.

Enriched Profiles

Applications can add to a User’s Reputation in a Portable and Composable way which can be displayed on Profiles and Avatars.

Gamification

A combination of Badges, XP and Levels can be used to gamify DAOs and Platforms.

Merit Badges

Badges to acknowledge and track members’ Proof of Contribution, Participation, and Engagement.

Loyalty Program

A Dynamic Loyalty Points System that gets triggered based on actions.
