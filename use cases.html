Quest Protocol

Quest Protocol is a customizable and extendible Framework that enables Developers and third-party applications to build Cross-Chain & Composable Reputation Systems for their Dapps.

Quest Platform

Quest Platform is a no-code tool, built on top of Quest protocol, helping Communities & Companies to Record Member’s Contributions and use it to reward and incentivize members.

Quest Protocol Modules

Using Quest Protocol's customizable Modules, Design & Deploy a Reputation System that works for your community to increase User Loyalty and Engagenment for your Community or Platform

Quest Platform

Issue Badges that contain XP, Verifiable Credentials and Skill-Score that then get added to a User's Profile and their Community Dynamic NFT. Based on Badges and XP, set Levels and issue rewards to members.

Use Cases

Our Use Cases are applicable to Web3 Communities, Platforms, Protocols & Networks, Games and Metaverses.
Gating

Grant Privileges such as gated access to content, channels, links and events based on Badges held or XP Scores.

Privileges

Roles and Permissions can be assigned to users based on Reputation accrued in a Community and on PLatforms.

Enriched Profiles

Applications can add to a User’s Reputation in a Portable and Composable way which can be displayed on Profiles and Avatars.

Gamification

A combination of Badges, XP and Levels can be used to gamify DAOs and Platforms.

Merit Badges

Badges to acknowledge and track members’ Proof of Contribution, Participation, and Engagement.

Loyalty Program
